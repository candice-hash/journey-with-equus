Journey With Equus exists to raise awareness around the gifts horses have to give beyond the saddle — through equine partnered wellness. These are horses, mules, donkeys, and ponies who, due to age, health, or injury, can no longer be ridden or compete. They come to us. They find safety. They heal. And in return, they help humans heal too. Every animal here receives a lifetime of sanctuary, love, and purpose. Your sponsorship makes that possible.
